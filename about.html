Welcome to Aurora

Where every scoop is a story.

Aurora Ice Cream is a premium ice cream brand established in 2016, inspired by the beauty of authentic Italian gelato and crafted with a modern vision. We believe every scoop should be an experience — bringing together exceptional ingredients, creative flavors, and unforgettable moments.

From timeless classics to signature Middle Eastern creations, Aurora offers handcrafted gelato made with passion, precision, and premium quality. Whether you're enjoying a single scoop, celebrating a special occasion, or creating memories with family and friends, Aurora transforms every visit into something extraordinary.

Our Story

Born from a simple dream.

Aurora was born from a simple dream: to redefine the ice cream experience. Inspired by the craftsmanship of traditional Italian gelato and the rich flavors of the Middle East, Aurora was founded in 2016 with a vision to create more than just desserts — we create moments of happiness.

Our journey began with a passion for combining authentic recipes, premium ingredients, and innovative flavor combinations. Every recipe is carefully developed to deliver a perfectly balanced texture, rich taste, and memorable experience. Today, Aurora continues to craft ice cream that celebrates creativity, quality, and the joy of sharing life's sweetest moments.

Our Philosophy

Exceptional gelato begins with exceptional ingredients.

We carefully select premium dairy, fresh fruits, fine chocolates, roasted nuts, and authentic natural flavors to create products that are smooth, rich, and full of character. Every batch is crafted with care to ensure consistency, freshness, and excellence in every scoop.

Our Mission

To create premium ice cream that brings people together through exceptional quality, innovative flavors, and memorable experiences.

Our Vision

To become the leading premium gelato brand in the Middle East, recognized for creativity, craftsmanship, and unforgettable customer experiences while expanding Aurora to destinations around the world.
